Dr. Dhan Singh Rawat

Minister of Higher Education, Govt of Uttarakhand

Dr. Dhan Singh Rawat is a Cabinet Minister in the Uttarakhand government and represents the Srinagar Garhwal constituency. Born in a remote village in Pauri Garhwal, he completed his early education locally and went on to earn a master’s and PhD in History from Hemwati Nandan Bahuguna Garhwal University.

Pushkar Singh Dhami

Chief Minister, Uttarakhand

Pushkar Singh Dhami is an Indian politician and a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party, serving as the Chief Minister of Uttarakhand since 4 July 2021. He represents the Champawat Assembly constituency in the Uttarakhand Legislative Assembly since 2022 and previously from Khatima from 2012 to 2022. He lost his seat in 2022 elections, but was re-elected as Chief Minister by BJP MLAs. He was later elected to Uttarakhand Legislative Assembly following a by-election. He is the only Chief Minister of the state to assume a second consecutive term as CM, since its creation
