Devbhumi Startup Expo

The Devbhumi Startup Expo will be a special platform where entrepreneurs and startups can present their ideas, products, and innovations to teachers, students, dignitaries, and guests attending the event.

    Recognition:
  • Top 3 startups will be awarded for their innovation, social impact, and business potential
  • Other participants will receive certificates of appreciation.
  • Special mention will be given to women entrepreneurs and rural startups doing inspiring work.
